City Description

Kotor is a charming coastal town in Montenegro, located at the end of a scenic bay surrounded by towering mountains. It is a UNESCO World Heritage Site, known for its well-preserved medieval architecture and fortifications

Kotor offers a variety of attractions for visitors, from exploring the narrow streets and squares of the Old Town, to climbing the steep stairs to the San Giovanni Fortress, which offers stunning views of the bay and the town

Kotor is also a paradise for cat lovers, as the town is home to many stray cats that are cared for by locals and tourists alike.
